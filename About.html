Our vision


Sustacon aims to be the preferred partner for businesses seeking long-term value while respecting the environment. We focus on creating thriving work environments where employees excel and providing practical, impactful advice in strategy, organizational development, and sustainability. With strong academic ties, we also offer young talents valuable learning and growth opportunities.

Why work with us

Engaged


We are not mercenaries. We only work for companies with whom we match and can only deliver our best results if there is mutual sympathy and trust between the management and us. This is how our unique and fine long-term relationships with our clients are

Added value


We guarantee that our advice is carefully underpinned so that our projects add value and margin to your organisation. We adapt to each context and ensure that advice is given in the client's language and culture.

Love brand


We consciously choose to be a boutique consultancy, where everyone is involved with the client. This is because we get energy from working with you and among your people. This is why you will love us.

Idealistic


We believe that companies can provide solutions to many social and environmental problems when they address them from their core expertise and with a scalable business case. We want to help you do that!

Prof. dr. Hans Verboven
Founder Sustacon


Hans studied literature & philosophy and economics in Leuven, Brussels, Heidelberg and London. He holds a PhD in Literature & Philosophy (Heidelberg). Hans is director of Sustacon and part-time professor at the Faculty of Business and Economics of the University of Antwerp. He has extensive board experience. Until 2019, he was Executive Director of EY Climate Change & Sustainability Services Belgium.

Hans has almost 15 years of experience in devising and improving processes and strategies, sustainability reporting and communication projects in Flemish and international companies, from SMEs to large family businesses. He enjoys working at boardroom level as much as on the factory floor.

Since 2017, Hans and his team support companies in sustainable growth, more margin and more impact under the brand name Sustacon.

Hans has a passion for construction, Italian food, the seaside, surfing, art and literature and speaks 6 languages. He lives in Oosterloo (Geel), in the most southern part of the 'Kempen'. He is married and has three children.
